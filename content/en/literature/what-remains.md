![Raindrops on a window](/images/lit-remains.jpg)

<p class="inline-image-caption">The less there is left, the more the words “I love you” become a last option rather than a lighter consolation.</p>

<div class="poem">
<p>Perhaps what matters most<br>
is that tonight, too, a star, on the wind,<br>
against us,<br>
grazes past.</p>

<p>Was that really<br>
more important than a heart that sings of the stars?</p>

<p>We are, after all,<br>
just a pale blue dot.</p>

<p>There is no helping it:<br>
nothing left but to love.</p>
</div>
