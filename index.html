Industry Experience

Over 7+ years of hands-on experience in lithium-ion battery electrode manufacturing — leading process optimization, production scale-up, and data-driven standardization across multiple production sites.

Data-Driven Manufacturing

Bridged the gap between production-floor data and engineering decisions using MES, RTDB, and process analytics to drive measurable improvements.

  • KPI dashboards connecting MES/RTDB to real-time process monitoring — +15% operational productivity (SK On)
  • Data-driven decision framework for mixing process control, replacing experience-based adjustments
  • Validated MES–ERP data integrity across production systems for reliable yield analysis

Electrode Process Engineering

Designed and optimized core electrode processes — slurry preparation, coating, calendering, slitting — for consistent quality at scale.

  • Mixing process standardization: improved silicon dosing precision via equipment calibration & SOPs (SK On, Seosan)
  • Optimized CNT slurry mixing — −15% viscosity variation, −10% defect rate (SKOY, Yancheng)
  • Developed LFP/NCM dual-line production strategy for next-gen chemistries

Production Scale-up

Supported new line launches and stabilization — from equipment commissioning to mass-production readiness — across Korea and China.

  • Led C-Sample production readiness for Polestar at SKOY Yancheng (DOE, integration, validation)
  • Built lines for premium EV customers (Polestar, Ferrari) — +25% production efficiency

Cross-functional System Integration

Worked at the intersection of MES, ERP, PLM, and QMS to ensure seamless data flow and traceability across the electrode production chain.

  • Standardized process documentation — −20% cross-department communication errors (SK On)
  • Diagnosed and resolved MES–ERP–RMS integration issues during line ramp-up
  • Contributed to SK On's integrated manufacturing & operations manual design
